The official Fight-Club rules

1. Main constitution:


§1.1. Fight Club (FC) is a group of friends, with the aim to fight bigger groups, clans and alliances.
§1.2.1. The group moral is based in respect, fairness and kindness.
§1.2.2. The group is made for a fun and enjoyable game which means that drama about the game is undesired and unnecessary.
§1.2.3. All members should respect each other and keep justice against enemies.
§1.3. To keep the server interesting after takeover, it‘s allowed and desired to build groups and make internal fights. These fights have to be fair and should be construed by arbitrary of the players.
§1.4. Members of fc are allowed and welcomed to use one of these symbols in their name: ‚öî‚Ñ±ùìí & ‚öî·∂†·∂ú & ‰πâ·∂†·∂ú.
§1.4.1. The meanings of the symbols is different, but all symbols have the same rank.
§1.4.2. ‚öî stands for the fight and the internal war. It contains the will to fight and to win.
§1.4.3. ‚Ñ±ùìí and ·∂†·∂ú stand for Fight-Club.
§1.4.4. ‰πâ is the chinese symbol for justice and means the same. Who wears it wants to bring justice in the game.
§1.5. It‘s not allowed to team up with people outside the tag, while playing with fc. That means to kill all.
§1.5.1. This rule excludes all kinds of fans and members of the group which don‘t use mod, as well as trustful helpers that shouldn't be betrayed.
§1.5.2. It‘s allowed to join against fc and eat members.
§1.5.3. It‘s not allowed to join an opponent group, when the opponent group is bigger and dominating.
§1.5.3.1. rule 1.5.3. is not mandatory if FC attacks a server where a member is already playing with another group. The member who plays with another group is then allowed to choose which groups he wants to help.
§1.5.3.2. Joining against fc still means to play with respect and fairness. That means especially not to savage.
§1.6. The recommended number of players for a server is 3-6.
§1.6.1. If more than 6 people join one server they should make sure that it is necessary.
§1.6.1.1. It is necessary when the opponent team has 10 teammates or more.
§1.6.1.2. It is necessary when the opponent team keeps savaging and there is no way to gain mass.
§1.6.1.3. It is not necessary when the server is already taken.
§1.6.1.4. It is not allowed when the opponent group has less than 6 teammates.
§1.6.2. if more than 6 people join when it is not necessary, it should be send a new server and split the teams.
§1.7. All information concerning the Fight-Club is secret. That especially includes the tag, in game names and conversations inside the group. It‘s not allowed to distribute these information without any persmission of an admin.
§1.8. It‘s not allowed to add new people inside the group without permission and asking the group before.
§1.8.1. To invite non-members in the game it‘s mandatory to change the tag.
§1.8.2. The main tag for playing with non-members is „FUNNY“
§1.8.3. The players have to agree with the invitation before an outstandind player gets invited and tag changes.
§1.9. Solo players should be respected and not getting killed.
§1.9.1. It is allowed to kill solo players when the solos attack the team or when it is the only way to save the team mass.
§1.9.2. It‘s not allowed to savage solo players.
§1.9.3. It is allowed to kill solo players, when they are shooting viruses or harm the team in another way.
§1.10. The group consists of members and admins.

 

§2. Member constitution


§2.1. Member is everyone in the group.
§2.2. Every member has the same rights and responsibilities.
§2.3. Members should be active at least once in 2 weeks.
§2.3.1. If a member is less active, he gets kicked from the group.
§2.3.2. It is possible to file an application to be less active.
§2.4. Every member can suggest new ideas for the group and can initiate a vote about it.
§2.5. Members that dont follow the rules get a warning or banned out of the group.
§2.6. The group is based on a proportional representation. That means the voting majority decides about the happenings in the group. Example: If 30% vote for yes, 20% for no and 50% abstain, then the 30% won.
§2.7. Becoming member happens, when the interested candidate agreed to the rules, the majority of admins agrees and the group is not against adding.
§2.7.1. It‘s an requirement for a new member to play good and understand the game.
§2.7.2. Exceptions can be made by admins.

 

§3. Admin constitution


§3.1. Admins are members that got voted by majority of the group as admins and be inaugurated by 100% agreement of all other admins.
§3.2. Admins have a veto right for every decision in the group.
§3.3. Admins should only act after they got permission of other admins.
§3.3.1. It‘s allowed to act without permission when it‘s necessary to act quickly in pressure, especially when the common weal and the structure of the group are endangered.
§3.3.2. it‘s allowed to act without permission when it‘s coinciding with the veto right.
§3.4. Admins have to secure the implementation of the rules and the group consistence.
§3.5. An admin decision is made, when the relative number of agreements is higher than disagreements.
§3.5.1. The admin decision can‘t be implemented if the majority of the group is against it or another admin avoids it by veto.
§3.5.2. If there is no clear result for a discussion about a decision in the group, the admins have exclusive right to decide.
§3.5.3. A decision can‘t be implemented if there is no clear result between the admin discussion.
§3.5.4. Admins are the main responible persons for recruiting new members.
§3.5.5. An admin can be deposed when all other admins agree with it or the majority of the group.
§3.5.6. An admin can be deposed immediately when he endangers the structural organization and the common weal of the group.
